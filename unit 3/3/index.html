  • What is the box model?

    • A Box Model is either a square or rectangular shape around images or texts or other elements, it has different properties that can be manipulated using CSS

  • What is the difference between margin and padding?

    • Padding is the inner space between the content and the border whereas Margin is the space between the border and other elements, it's whitespace available surrounding an element.

  • What is collapsing margin?

    • its the top and bottom margins that are sometimes collapsed into a single margin that makes it equal to the larger of the two margins.

  • What is the purpose of the box-sizing CSS property?

    • it allows us to define how the overall, total width and height of an element is calculated

  • What does the outline property do?

    • it goes around your border but doesn't take up space. Just an outline around your content.

    • by using background-image
